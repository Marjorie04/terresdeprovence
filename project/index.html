The Spa Les Terres de Provence


Enter an ocean of greenery, a bubble of serenity, far from the hustle and bustle of the world and the tumult of the cities and refocus on the essential in a unique setting, in the heart of a preserved nature... The Spa Terres de Provence answers your desires. You will appreciate the multiple possibilities of stays that we offer you, whether alone, as a couple, with friends or with your family!

Terres de Provence Spa combines traditional and modern therapies, innovative technologies and cosmetologies with aromatherapy specific to Provence.
